Pizza

Man's favourite dish

You make the base, add tomato sauce, the toppings and then send it to the oven!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Tomato sauce
  • Toppings of choice

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, salt and water and the yeast.
  2. Let rest for 30 minutes to an hour.
  3. Gently make the base into a pizza shape.
  4. Add tomato sauce all over.
  5. Add toppings.
  6. Bake and enjoy.
